ABOUT US


HELPERS CALL aims to create a healthy, helpful and efficient system of exchanging work and services

between Clients( people who need help with their chores and services) and Helpers( people who can provide

the service and complete the chore).

People can sign up as a Helper,Client or both depending on their personal choice.

Clients can put up their chores and Helpers will put up the prices for which they will complete the chore

and we will make sure that the Client gets the best(cheapest) offer.

We hope you enjoy your time with us!
